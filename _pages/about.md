# 👋 About Me

I am **Yuer Gao**, a Ph.D. student at [The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou)](https://www.hkust-gz.edu.cn/),
advised by [**Prof. ADVISOR_NAME**](https://ADVISOR_HOMEPAGE).

### 🪸 CoralPlan &nbsp;<small>· Language-Guided Underwater Task Planning</small>

![sym](../images/coralplan_teaser.gif)

* Bringing **language-guided task planning** to underwater robots: given a high-level instruction
  (e.g., *"survey the bleached corals in this region"*), the robot decomposes it into actionable
  sub-tasks and executes them with closed-loop feedback in dynamic marine environments.
* `Yuer Gao`, Co-author A, Co-author B, **Advisor Name**.  *In Submission / In Preparation*.
  &nbsp;[[Project Page]](#) [[Code]](#) [[Video]](#)

### 🧠 CoralVLM &nbsp;<small>· Vision-Language Reasoning for Coral Health</small>

![sym](../images/coralvlm_teaser.gif)

* A **vision-language model specialized for coral reef understanding** — fine-tuned on curated
  underwater imagery to reason about species, bleaching levels, and structural complexity from a
  single frame, enabling scalable ecosystem monitoring without expert annotation.
* `Yuer Gao`, Co-author A, **Advisor Name**.  *In Preparation*.
  &nbsp;[[Project Page]](#) [[Code]](#)

---

### 🎯 Active Underwater Reconstruction &nbsp;<small>· Uncertainty-Aware Viewpoint Planning</small>

![sym](../images/active_recon_teaser.gif)

* An **active perception** framework that drives an underwater robot to plan its next viewpoint
  by reasoning about reconstruction uncertainty. The result is high-quality dense maps of complex
  marine scenes (corals, wrecks, kelp forests) with far fewer captures than passive scanning.
* `Yuer Gao`, Co-author A, **Advisor Name**.  *RA-L 2025 (accepted)*.
  &nbsp;[[Paper]](#) [[Code]](#) [[Video]](#)

---

### 🌊 Marine Gaussian Splatting &nbsp;<small>· High-Fidelity Underwater Scene Reconstruction</small>

![sym](../images/marine_gs_teaser.gif)

* Adapting **3D Gaussian Splatting** to the underwater domain, accounting for scattering,
  attenuation, and turbidity. Produces photorealistic novel-view synthesis of coral reefs and
  underwater structures from short ROV trajectories.
* `Yuer Gao`, Co-author A, **Advisor Name**.  *In Submission*.
